**About OpenAI**

OpenAI is a nonprofit organization that aims to promote the development and application of artificial intelligence (AI) for the benefit of humanity.

**Key Findings:**

* OpenAI was founded in 2015 as a nonprofit organization, but transitioned to a for-profit company in 2019.
* The company has grown rapidly, with $4.9 billion in revenue in 2024 and a valuation of $157 billion.
* OpenAI's ChatGPT has become a dominant force in the chatbot market, with $2.9 billion in annual recurring revenue (ARR) as of September 2024.
* The company has partnered with Microsoft, Apple, and other organizations to advance the development of AI.
* OpenAI has faced challenges, including controversy surrounding the premature release of GPT-4, fund mismanagement, and internal conflicts, which led to the ouster of CEO Sam Altman.
* Despite these challenges, OpenAI remains committed to promoting the development and application of AI for the benefit of humanity.

**Management:**

* Key employees include Sam Altman, CEO; Jason Weston, Director of Research; and Stephen Pinker, Chief Scientist.
* The company has a board of directors that includes prominent figures such as Elon Musk, Reid Hoffman, and Andrew Ng.
* Principal individual investors include Sam Altman, Elon Musk, and Reid Hoffman.
